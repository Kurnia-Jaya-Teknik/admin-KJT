HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. Navigate to: http://localhost/admin-KJT/public/admin/cuti

2. Click "Lihat Detail" on any cuti

3. In modal, view employee info & cuti details

4. Click "Buat Surat" (if status = Disetujui)

5. Confirm in dialog

6. Wait 5-15 seconds for PDF generation

7. Click "Lihat Surat" to preview

8. Click "Download" to save PDF
